Inspired by One Little Dachshund

Hi, I’m Bailee, the founder of ByPablo.


The brand is named after our much-loved Miniature Dachshund Pablo, who has been the inspiration behind ByPablo from the very beginning. His playful personality, love for adventure and everyday antics inspired me to create a collection of high quality, practical dog products that are designed for everyday life while still looking great.


At ByPablo, we believe every adventure is better with your best friend by your side. Every product is carefully selected with comfort, quality and functionality in mind.
